Refund and Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation Terms

Once we have finalized the project details, including the project completion date and scope, the cancellation period is only available within 48 hours before the agreed-upon completion date. You may change or cancel the project details at any time up to 48 hours prior to the deadline. However, once the 48-hour mark has passed, no further changes or cancellations can be made. This is to ensure timely delivery of our services and to avoid disruptions in our workflow.

2. Watermark Version and Final Delivery

Upon project completion, we will send you a watermark version of the final product to ensure that the work meets your expectations. Once we receive the payment, we will send you the original version of the project without the watermark. This process ensures that you are satisfied with the completed work before making the final payment.

3. Delays in Project Delivery

In the event that we are unable to submit the project by the agreed-upon completion date, we kindly request that you grant us an additional 48 hours to complete and deliver the project. If the project is not submitted within that extended period, we will not charge any fees for the project. We strive to meet deadlines, but unforeseen circumstances may cause delays. In such cases, we will inform you promptly and work diligently to complete the project.

4. Communication and Changes

We encourage open communication during the project. If you wish to request changes or modifications, please do so within the allowed cancellation period or at any time before the final delivery. Once the project is finalized and the payment is processed, no further changes will be made unless specifically agreed upon in writing.

5. Refunds

Refunds are only applicable in cases where the project has not been completed as agreed, or if significant delays have occurred that cannot be resolved. Please contact us immediately if you have any concerns regarding the progress of the project. We will work with you to resolve any issues to your satisfaction.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Refund and Cancellation Policy periodically. Any updates will be reflected on this page. Please review the policy regularly to stay informed about any changes.

If you have any questions or need further clarification regarding this policy, please feel free to contact us.
