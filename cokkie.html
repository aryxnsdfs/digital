Cookie Policy

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (such as your computer or mobile phone) when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons, including:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly, allowing you to navigate and use our site’s features.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting data anonymously, which helps us improve our site's functionality.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies help us remember your preferences for a personalized experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies track your activity to help deliver relevant ads to you.

3. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through your settings. You can choose to delete or block cookies, but doing so may impact your experience on our website.

To learn more about cookie settings, check your browser’s settings or visit the help section of your web browser.

4. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page periodically for the latest information on our cookie practices.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us.
